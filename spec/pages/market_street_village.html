Living at Market Street Village

Here at Market Street Village, you'll have all the benefits of living downtown without sacrificing convenience. Our hip downtown location means you're able to walk to nearby parks, festivals and events, hitting up all of San Diego's hotspots on foot or the nearby trolley line. Whoever said downtown apartments are small hasn't been to Market Street Village, where you'll have a fully equipped kitchen complete with modern stainless steel appliances, walk-in closets, oversized windows, an in-home washer and dryer and a rooftop deck that's perfect for hosting friends and family for dinner. Topped with community amenities like our media room with theater seating, heated swimming pool, business center and clubhouse, you've never experienced downtown living like that at Market Street Village.

Downtown San Diego

Market Street Village Apartments are located in East Village, the fastest-growing neighborhood in Downtown San Diego. If walkability is important to you, you'll love our trendy location near the Gaslamp Quarter! There's no shortage of entertainment, whether you're in the mood to catch a game or concert at Petco Park, browse the art in local galleries, or party in the streets at one of the neighborhood's seasonal festivals. East Village offers its residents all the perks of living downtown without sacrificing convenience, so make sure to stop by to see our community and neighborhood for yourself!
