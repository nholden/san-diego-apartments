Living at Vantage Pointe

Vantage Pointe Apartments is sure to take your breath away, if not with the stunning views from our sky-high homes, then with the community and apartment amenities that make this a place like none other. Our downtown location allows you to walk just about anywhere you need to go, from nearby transit lines to grocery stores and schools, and our rooftop terrace is a sight not to be missed. Take the edge off in our 24 Hour fitness center and spin room, and bask in the sun in our 10th-floor swimming pool. Complete with an in-home washer and dryer, hard surface flooring, a private balcony and open-concept kitchens with top-notch finishes, Vantage Pointe is truly the ultimate in luxury downtown living.

Downtown San Diego

Vantage Pointe is a high-rise apartment building in Upper East Village, Downtown San Diego's largest neighborhood. Although the area is known for its walkability and proximity to just about anything you could want, East Village is home to several trolley and bus lines that make commuting a breeze. Whether you're looking to soak up some sun at nearby Coronado Beach, have dinner and drinks in the Gaslamp Quarter or take a walk along the bay, East Village has all the bases covered. Take a look at the map to see locals' recommendations, and schedule a trip to see the vibrant neighborhood for yourself!
